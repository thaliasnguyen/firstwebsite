Design Elements

Contrast

Typography
Typography is a good representation of contrast.
The website uses color and font choices to create contrast.
In areas where the same fonts are used, square shapes and colors are used to separate sections.
Dark backgrounds use a white font color and lighter backgrounds use black font color.

Repetition

  • Be_journey is a good representation of repetition on a website.
  • The website uses a teal color palette that grabs viewer’s attention.
  • The same teal color is used through the website with different shapes to separate areas.

Alignment

  1. Kyle Steed is a good demonstration of alignment.
  2. Photographs of Kyle Steed’s work are displayed on a white background in squares and rectangles that fill the entire page.
  3. The photos are designed to stretch and/or multiply in columns and rows.
  4. They align with how big the browser is to ensure no gaps between the photos.

Proximity

Oh Joy!
Oh Joy! uses proximity and white space to create an organized layout.
The website organizes similar elements into a group so viewers can easily navigate areas.
Blue titles and black descriptions and dates with different fonts do a good job of differentiating each part.
